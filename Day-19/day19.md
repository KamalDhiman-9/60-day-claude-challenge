### 💡 Key Learnings
- Data-driven football predictions combine multiple signals: historical win rates, current form scores, and live tournament results often tell different (and sometimes contradictory) stories — e.g., Spain's strong pre-tournament form vs. their shaky 0-0 opener.
- A personality-to-player match works best when it triangulates several traits (leadership, decision style, team orientation, work ethic) rather than a single question — small differences across traits produced a close but distinguishable 61% vs 54% split.
- Being a "Casual Viewer" (40/100 awareness) doesn't mean lacking football knowledge — it highlighted a specific gap (precise terminology and reading close statistical margins) rather than a broad weakness.
